How this POC works
  1. You describe the service you need, your location, and rough budget.
  2. The VendorMatch agent searches the curated dataset and surfaces the most relevant vendors.
  3. You review details like location, rating, and pricing — a future companion agent will handle calendar‑aware booking and follow‑ups.
